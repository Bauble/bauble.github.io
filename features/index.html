Taxonomy

We make every effort to maintain the relationships between your taxonomic groupings. You shouldn't have to worry about mispelled plant names and obsolete synonyms.

Accessions

Good accession data is the heart of any good plant collections. Bauble comes with rich support for collection data, verifications, vouchers, plant status and health.

Propagations

Maintain full propagation records for your plant collections. Even maintain propagations of your propagations and keep track of a plants clonal history throughout the lifetime of your garden.

Backups

Your data is backed up securely multiple times a day. All backups are encrypted and fully redundant so you shouldn't have to worry about the safety of your invaluable data.

...so much more

Bauble does everything you would expect from a professional plant collection system with the added benefit of working on your mobile device, being hosted in the cloud and making sure that your plant records are ready for the 21st century.
